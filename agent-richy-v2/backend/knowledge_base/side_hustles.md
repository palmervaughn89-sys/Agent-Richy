# Side Hustles and Extra Income

## Why Extra Income Matters
The average American household spends $72,967/year (2024 BLS). Side income can:
- Accelerate emergency fund building
- Pay off debt 2-3x faster
- Fund Roth IRA without touching primary income
- Build skills and career safety net

## Side Hustle Tax Rules
- Side income over $400/year requires filing Schedule SE (self-employment tax: 15.3%)
- Track ALL expenses for deductions: mileage ($0.70/mile in 2025), home office, supplies, software
- Set aside 25-30% of side income for taxes
- Consider quarterly estimated tax payments to avoid penalties

## From Side Hustle to Business
1. Validate the idea (make $1,000 first)
2. Track income and expenses from day one
3. Open a separate bank account
4. Get proper insurance if needed
5. Consider LLC for liability protection
6. Scale by raising prices before adding hours

## Financial Rules for Side Income
1. **100% to debt** if you have high-interest debt
2. **100% to emergency fund** until it's fully funded
3. **After that:** 50% investing, 30% fun, 20% skill building
4. **Never increase lifestyle** based on side hustle income (unless it's consistent 6+ months)
