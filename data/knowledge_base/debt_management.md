# Debt Management

## Types of Debt

### Good Debt (Low Interest, Builds Value)
- Mortgage (typically 6-7.5% in 2025)
- Student loans (federal: 5.5% for undergrad, 7% for grad in 2025)
- Business loans for proven concepts

### Bad Debt (High Interest, No Asset)
- Credit cards (average APR: 22-28% in 2025)
- Payday loans (300-500%+ APR — avoid at all costs)
- Personal loans for consumption (8-36% APR)
- Buy Now Pay Later with missed payments

## Debt Payoff Strategies

### The Avalanche Method (Mathematically Optimal)
Pay minimum on everything, throw all extra at the HIGHEST interest rate debt.
- Saves the most money in total interest
- Can feel slow if your highest-rate debt is also your largest balance
- Best for disciplined, numbers-driven people

### The Snowball Method (Psychologically Powerful)
Pay minimum on everything, throw all extra at the SMALLEST balance debt.
- Quick wins build motivation
- You may pay more total interest
- 70% completion rate vs ~50% for avalanche
- Best for people who need motivation wins

### Debt Consolidation
Combine multiple debts into one payment, ideally at a lower interest rate.
- Balance transfer cards: 0% APR for 12-21 months (3-5% transfer fee)
- Personal consolidation loan: Usually 8-15% APR
- Only works if you DON'T rack up new debt on the freed-up cards

## Debt Priority Ladder
1. Make minimum payments on ALL debts (always)
2. Get employer 401(k) match (free money)
3. Pay off high-interest debt (>7% APR) aggressively
4. Build emergency fund to 3-6 months
5. Pay medium-interest debt (5-7%) OR invest (roughly equal)
6. Low-interest debt (< 5%) can coexist with investing

## Debt-to-Income Ratio
- Monthly debt payments ÷ gross monthly income × 100
- Under 20%: Healthy
- 20-35%: Manageable
- 36-49%: Struggling
- 50%+: Crisis — seek help

## When to Seek Professional Help
- Debt-to-income ratio over 50%
- Considering bankruptcy
- Being sued by creditors
- Unable to make minimum payments
Contact: National Foundation for Credit Counseling (NFCC.org) for free or low-cost counseling.
